Cracking Internet Explorer's Content advisor
TARGET = Internet Explorer
DIFF = 2/10
TOOLS USED = SoftICE
Proceeding 
     Cracking Internet Explorer version 6.0.2600.0000's Content Advisor
                                         by BoR0


Many people lost their password on iexplore's content advisor (I did, 
too!). 
Prolly for every program there is a solution, so for this one there's a 
solution too ;)

Lets get going! Ok, fire up iexplore.exe. Click on Tools, Internet 
Explorer and then click on Content.

Press Ctrl-D to fire soft-ice up. Once its fired, set a breakpoint on 
GetDlgItemTextA (bpx getdlgitemtexta).

(The GetDlgItemTextA is used for most programs, its checking what you 
typed in the Editbox)

Ok, press Ctrl-D to exit softice, and press on Settings now.

Internet Explorer asks for password. Just type any password and press 
OK.

Softice fires up, now do this:

Press F11 to get the caller, to see where this comes from.
Type: "A 7040B9CA" press enter and type "JZ 7040B9D3".
(This changes the JNZ 7040B9D3 to JZ 7040B9D3 at address 7040B9CA.)

Press ENTER and then ESCAPE.

After that type bd * to disable our breakpoints
(bc will clear them, bd will disable them, you can enable them by 
typing be * and if you want to list them you type bl)

You have entered the hive ;)
Whats next? 


Click on General tab, and press on Change Password button. OK, a screen 
appears which asks for:

- Old password
- New password
- Confirm new password

Fire up softice, and type "be *" to enable our breakpoint. After that, 
close softice again. Now on Old Password editbox type some non-sense 
password, on new password editbox type the password you want to use and 
on second's editbox password type it again. Press OK and softice fires 
itselfs up. Press F5 to quit softice (because it breaks on our first 
editbox, we dont want to change our jump on our first editbox, we want to 
do it on our second), F11 to get the caller and now type this:

"A 70405953" press enter and type "JZ 7040595F"
(you can scroll up and see what A means)

After doing that, press ENTER then ESCAPE, and type "bc *" to clear our 
breakpoint. OK, we have cleared our breakpoints, now we will exit 
softice by pressing Ctrl+D.

Internet Explorer says: "Password changed successfully."

Great! We have cracked Internet Explorer ;)
